## Summary

The milestone goal is complete: `optimusctx init` now leads supported-client onboarding with user-facing intent and destination choices, shows exact targets before mutation, and reports outcomes with materially less write-path noise.

All eight milestone requirements are satisfied on the repository branch. The changes integrate cleanly with the supported-client backend from `v1.3.1` and the same-command interactive path from `v1.3.2`, preserve the direct non-interactive `init --client <client> [--write]` contract, and passed both automated regression coverage and real PTY walkthroughs in a disposable repository.

The older manual Claude-host validation item from `v1.3.1` remains unchanged and outside the scope of this milestone. It does not reflect a gap in the `v1.3.3` implementation or verification surface.
